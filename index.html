Who We Are


DOATV is an online marketplace and SaaS platform for digital out of homes (OOH) display board for the purpose of advertising, broadcasting and entertainment, with a focus on simplifying the planning and booking of OOH display boards, thereby supercharging its efficiency and connectivity. DOATV mobile app effectively connect Users (brand/media owners)to OOH boards, creating new market opportunities.
